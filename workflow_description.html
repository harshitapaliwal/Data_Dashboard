WorkFlow Description

Data Dashboard describes data of students in the form of charts (pie and bar chart) and table so it helps recruiters to decide which colleges sholud be considered in next hiring session and which colleges to be dropped off based on the following criteria

  • Percentage of students hired in previous hiring session
  • Average CGPA
  • Test score in general aptitude test

The Dashboard also gives information about candidates who performed best in certain technology by giving average test scores from a college and also the average number of students from a particular stream and college.

The dashboard allows you to take a look at data of previous years (By using year filter buttons at the top) which plays a major role for the recruiter to take the decision.

The five colleges whose data being displayed are

  1. IIT Madras
  2. IIT Bombay
  3. IIT Kharagpur
  4. VIT University
  5. Anna University

The first is Pie Chart which displays data about percentage of students Hired Last Year from a particular college

Second is bar chart which displays data about Average CGPA of students of particular college

Third bar chart displays average test scores of students in a particular technology from a college.
Technologies considered are Java, C and DotNet

Fourth bar chart displays average percentage of students in a particular stream i.e ECE, CSE, IT from different colleges

Fifth bar chart displays average test score of students in General Aptitude from a particular college

The data is also displayed in the form of table for refrence
